# Privacy

Metis Orchestrator is local-first, and privacy is not a setting here, it is the architecture.

## What we collect

Nothing. Metis has no account system, no servers of its own, no analytics, and no telemetry. We do not track you, we do not use cookies, and there is no third-party analytics anywhere in the app.

## Where your data lives

On your machine. Your conversations, projects, gallery boards, routing policies, and API keys are stored locally in the app's data directory on your own device. They are never uploaded to us, because there is nowhere for them to go.

## When data leaves your device

Only in two cases, both of which you control:

1. **When you route a task to a cloud provider you configured.** If you send a prompt to Anthropic, OpenAI, Google, DeepSeek, Groq, NVIDIA, or OpenRouter, that prompt goes to that provider using your own API key, and it is handled under that provider's privacy policy, not ours. If you keep everything local (Ollama), nothing leaves your machine at all.
2. **When the app fetches the public marketplace.** Metis pulls its model catalog, marketplace packages, and Community feed from a public GitHub repository. These are ordinary read-only downloads. No personal data is sent as part of them.

## Your API keys

Your keys are stored locally on your device and are sent only to the matching provider when you make a request. They are never transmitted to us or to any third party.

## No telemetry

Metis reports no usage data to anyone. The only usage statistics that exist are the token and timing numbers shown to you inside the app, computed and stored locally.
